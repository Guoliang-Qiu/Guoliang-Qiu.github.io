About Me

I am Guoliang Qiu (邱国良), a researcher at Huawei Taylor Lab.

I earned my Ph.D. from the Basic Studies in Computing Science Lab (BASICS) at Shanghai Jiao Tong University in June 2025, where I was fortunate to be advised by Prof. Chihao Zhang.

I obtained my B.Eng. degree from the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China in June 2020, where I was advised by Prof. Mingyu Xiao.

Research Interest

My research interest lies broadly in theoretical computer science (TCS). Previously, I focus on counting & sampling and online combinatorial optimization.
